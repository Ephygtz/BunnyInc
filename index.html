About

Bunny Inc. Ltd.

  • Established in 2013

    We are one of the prominent travel, consultancy and event management companies in Kenya.

    The company was founded on the realization that there are many untapped opportunities in the travel industry and the desire to reduce the immense strain to settle suffered by clients from visiting countries who have a short timeline to accomplish so much.  As the industry has continued to grow, Bunny Inc. has risen to be the best by ensuring quality service delivery and success of our engagements.
    Our team of enthusiastic young staff has a passion for travel and socializing; they know what makes appointments successful and understand every element of value attainment. Our creative touch and wide network guarantees our clients a one-of-a-kind experience each time. Bunny Inc. Ltd, as a result, has acquired a fair share of clients who have been especially impressed by the expertise and professional services that we aim to provide continuously. The company’s primary focus is to meet our clients’ business, leisure and educational needs, surpassing their expectations.

  • Our Vision

    To be the leading consultancy, travel and event management company in the country, ensuring profitable growth of the client/organization and excellent return on capital employed by exceeding customer expectations!

  • Our Mission

    At Bunny Inc. Ltd, we realize that every appointment we undertake will have a bearing on our clients’ business and reputation. We are therefore dedicated to the development of our clients’ vision in the ever-changing world of client needs. Our staff works tirelessly to customize each projects by working within the clients budget to assure our clients extensive exposure. This is what makes us different!!

  • Our Understanding.

IDEO.Org.

Year 2013

IDEO.Org has been the backbone of our pride in field research, translation and logistics as they have been with us since 2013 and have been our valued clients to date, we have been involved in their projects to as far as Arusha in Tanzania, Kericho, Kisumu, Mombasa and in Machakos, Mwingi, Kibera, Kawangware, Korogocho, Mlolongo, Kahawa West, Kangemi just to note but a few.
IDEO.Org is non profit organization comprising of assorted world class designers; based in the US, San Francisco. They help in funding and creating ideas for community based organizations and start-ups that help alleviate poverty and improve livelihoods for people around the world, its an affiliate of the larger IDEO company that has been in existence for a couple of decades.

F.S.D Trust Kenya.

Year 2013

We were on recommendation by the IDEO.Org team on a previous appointment contracted by the FSD Trust Kenya for two weeks as consultants. Our role was to provide logistical solutions, recruit/Identify respondents for a field study and offer translation services, since the research was happening in slums, we provided field scouts who provided easy access routes within the slums and offered security to the research team.
FSD Trust Kenya is based in Upper-hill Nairobi, its actively involved in funding and investing in great ideas.

ATID International-Israel.

Year 2015-current.

ATID International is arguably the largest vocational training institute in ISRAEL, offering seminars and vocational training sessions to various countries around the globe and with experience in west, central Africa and in countries as near as Uganda.

ATID International is our newest project partner with whom we have established a partnership that will see us send students to Israel for further studies for an internationally recognized diploma within 2-3wks of study depending on the topic of study, this will be a first in Kenya.Its in our own interest to market Kenya as a hub by introducing educational tourism, the knowledge acquired by our kin will be used to develop our country under the devolved government that requires input from every citizen.
